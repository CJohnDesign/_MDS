ABOUT MEDISCAN 

WHO WE ARE

Since our inception, Mediscan has rapidly grown to become the premier employer of highly qualified school-based and healthcare professionals. Our employees are hand-selected to provide specialized services at healthcare facilities and school systems throughout the country. We’ve made it our mission to build our reputation on providing a personal touch to both our clients and candidates!

With offices in stunning Los Angeles, California as well as sunny Tampa, Florida, Mediscan's 80+ corporate employees consist of highly trained, personable recruitment managers, specialized HR, IT & Administration Coordinators, and Licensed Therapists. With our staff being supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and technology, Mediscan is uniquely positioned to provide multiple staffing and educational programs to school districts and medical facilities throughout the United States.

Our foundation is built upon our passion for making a difference. Our employees are the heart of our business. We invest heavily in our programs and processes to ensure that we make exceptional connections between our clients and employees. With offerings that help guide professionals through every stage of their careers, we are coaching a new generation of healthcare and school-based professionals. In turn, the Mediscan community is spreading its culture of care across the nation!  

OUR HISTORY

Established in 1995, Mediscan built a solid reputation of providing quality healthcare professionals to medical facilities throughout the state of California. As our organization grew, we began partnering with local school systems to supplement the hiring needs within the special education departments throughout the state of California. Over the years, our expertise in providing outstanding healthcare and school-based staffing services to our clients within California enabled us to evolve into a leading provider of highly qualified healthcare professionals to schools and healthcare facilities not only in the state of California, but throughout the country. 

In 2014, Mediscan, acquired DirectEd. DirectEd was founded in 2007 to provide substitute teachers who specialize in meeting the needs of charter schools in Los Angeles, California. By 2011, DirectEd added a special education agency to meet the significant staffing challenges being faced by charter schools in the area of special education. Today, DirectEd continues to grow by leaps and bounds, expanding employment opportunities into the San Diego, Sacramento, and Oakland, California.

In October 2015, Mediscan was acquired by Cross County Healthcare. Cross Country Healthcare is a renowned national leader of primary healthcare staffing and workforce solutions that assists more than 3,000 healthcare facilities in the United States and Caribbean. We continually endeavor to stride ahead of the curve in an ever-changing marketplace, producing innovative methods and quality services to improve continuity of patient care with cost-effective results.
The collaboration of our brands affords our employees access to provide services at client locations across multiple settings in locations throughout the United States and Caribbean. This enables our field employees to augment their experiences by building an incredibly robust résumé while building tenure under the same corporate umbrella. Our candidates will also enjoy maintaining the same benefits and service that you have grown accustom to as a Mediscan employee.

WHAT WE BELIEVE AND HOW WE ACHIEVE

PEOPLE

We are only as good as our employees: we invest in them.
We employ highly qualified, engaged professionals and staff, and recognize them as individuals with both personal and career goals. Our mission is to not only be the placement agency of choice for our professionals but also their career development center. We strive to build personal bonds with our employees and truly understand where they would like their career to lead.

SERVICE

We provide superior service to our candidates and clients.
Emphasizing integrity, commitment and excellence, we provide the highest quality service. We have attained 100% Joint Commission compliance and will continue to raise the bar in developing best practices in our industry. All of our professionals pass a rigorous certification process with a thorough background check, education verification, skills test and an in-depth interview before they are presented for any position to ensure only the best qualified candidates are placed in positons.

COLLABORATION

We are leading innovators in our field.
We believe in working with you to help you achieve your career goals. To us, our role is more than providing jobs. By sharing knowledge, learning and building consensus with our professionals, we can develop a deep and collective strategy for helping them achieve a fulfilling career.
