About Mediscan

Since our inception, Mediscan has rapidly grown to become the premier employer of highly qualified school-based and healthcare professionals. Our employees are hand-selected to provide specialized services at healthcare facilities and school systems throughout the country. We’ve made it our mission to build our reputation on providing a personal touch to both our clients and candidates!

With offices in stunning Los Angeles, California as well as sunny Tampa, Florida, Mediscan's 80+ corporate employees consist of highly trained, personable recruitment managers, specialized HR, IT & Administration Coordinators, and Licensed Therapists. With our staff being supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and technology, Mediscan is uniquely positioned to provide multiple staffing and educational programs to school districts and medical facilities throughout the United States.
